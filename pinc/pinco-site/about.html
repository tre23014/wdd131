About People in Need Coalition

Our Story

People in Need Coalition (PINC) began as a small group of volunteers determined to serve those experiencing hunger and homelessness in Idaho Falls. What started with just a few meals has grown into a powerful community movement providing consistent care, support, and dignity.

What We Do

Every week, we gather to provide free meals, offer community resources, and create a space of compassion for anyone in need. Our team is 100% volunteer-based, and our support comes directly from generous donors and local organizations.
